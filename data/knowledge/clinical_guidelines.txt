CLINICAL GUIDELINES FOR HOSPITAL READMISSION RISK

SOURCE: Synthetic clinical reference document for ClinicalMind RAG system.

READMISSION RISK FACTORS
========================

HIGH RISK INDICATORS (each factor significantly increases readmission probability):
- Prior admissions: 3 or more admissions in the past 12 months is a strong predictor
- No follow-up appointment: Patients discharged without scheduled follow-up are 2x more likely to readmit
- Lives alone: Lack of social support increases readmission risk by 40%
- Medication non-compliance history: Associated with 60% higher readmission rates
- Length of stay > 7 days: Indicates severe illness, higher readmission risk
- Age > 75: Elderly patients have significantly higher readmission rates
- Diagnosis: CHF, COPD, Sepsis, and AMI carry highest 30-day readmission rates

MEDIUM RISK INDICATORS:
- Prior admissions: 1-2 admissions in past 12 months
- Age 60-75
- Length of stay 4-7 days
- Diagnosis: Diabetes, CKD, Pneumonia

LOW RISK INDICATORS:
- No prior admissions
- Follow-up appointment scheduled
- Age under 60
- Length of stay under 3 days
- Strong social support documented

DIAGNOSIS-SPECIFIC READMISSION RATES (30-day):
- Congestive Heart Failure: 24%
- COPD: 22%
- Sepsis: 20%
- Acute Myocardial Infarction: 18%
- Pneumonia: 17%
- Hip Fracture: 15%
- Type 2 Diabetes: 14%
- Chronic Kidney Disease: 16%
- Hypertensive Crisis: 12%
- Lumbar Spinal Stenosis: 8%

INTERVENTION RECOMMENDATIONS:
- HIGH risk patients: Contact within 24 hours post-discharge, arrange home visit
- MEDIUM risk patients: Phone call within 72 hours, confirm follow-up appointment
- LOW risk patients: Standard discharge instructions, confirm follow-up

RISK SCORING FORMULA:
Points-based system:
- 3+ prior admissions: +3 points
- No follow-up: +3 points
- Lives alone: +2 points
- Non-compliant: +2 points
- LOS > 7 days: +2 points
- Age > 75: +2 points
- Age 60-75: +1 point
- High-risk diagnosis: +3 points
- Medium-risk diagnosis: +1 point

TOTAL SCORE INTERPRETATION:
- 7+ points: HIGH RISK
- 3-6 points: MEDIUM RISK
- 0-2 points: LOW RISK
